Some of my projects

SpotiFriend IOS App

Location based music discovery/playlist app. Users place custom markers on the world map, and can search for other playlist markers created by users. Playlist markers only appear if the user is in range of them, thus creates insentive to explore areas around the globe and discover their musical tastes. Each playlist is linked to a Spotify playlist, and when tapped will take the user to that playlist and begin playback. Created to learn IOS App development tools: xCode, Swift, and swiftUI.
